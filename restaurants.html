Restaurants

Pale Fire
Pale Fire Brewing offers up to 10 of their freshly made beers on tap along with other brew options from around Virginia. This trendy spot has an indoor outdoor layout with seats offered outside on the patio or inside at the bar or in the library. With an open food policy, this is a great place to bring your favorite meal and enjoy a tasty beer.

Beyond
Beyond is an Asian restaurant that offers a chic experience with sushi, fusion dishes, and cocktails.

Jimmy Madisons
Jimmy Madisons is located right in the center of town. Sit down at the bar with great music and premium whiskey, or dine upstairs on the patio that overlooks the town all while enjoying some great southern cuisine.

Jack Brown's
Jack Brown's offers a great variety of wings, burgers, finger food, and drinks. Sit down or take out from this rustic location right in downtown Harrisonburg.

Rocktown Kitchen
This popular farm-to-table restaurant offers plenty of elevated American cuisine options. If you're looking for a place to go just for a casual fine dining experience, this is the place for you! Although it isn't located right in the center of town, Rocktown Kitchen is certainly a hidden gem that has taken the dining experience to a whole new level.

Bella Gelato & Pasteries
Bella offers a wide variety of tasty treats and it is a great place to visit on a hot day for some gelato or if you are in the mood for a fresh pastery.
